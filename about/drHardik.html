Dr. Hardik V. Barot (BPT, MHM, LLB)

Founder & Lead Consultant – DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP

Dr. Hardik V. Barot is a qualified healthcare management professional with a unique blend of clinical, administrative, and legal expertise. After completing his Bachelor of Physiotherapy, he pursued a Master’s in Hospital Management and a Bachelor of Law to strengthen his multidisciplinary approach to healthcare leadership.

With over 8 years of experience, Dr. Barot has collaborated with more than 100 hospitals across Gujarat, leading numerous NABH accreditation and hospital planning projects. His consultancy has helped healthcare institutions enhance operational efficiency, ensure compliance, and achieve quality excellence.

As the Founder of DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP, he continues to guide hospitals in accreditation, planning, legal documentation, and process optimization — driven by a mission to elevate the standards of healthcare management in India.
