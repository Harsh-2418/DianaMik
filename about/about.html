About DianaMik Consultancy

DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy now DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP was found on 16th August 2018 by Dr Hardik V Barot, a qualified hospital management person. After completing his study in 2016 Dr Barot embarked on a career dedicated to enhancing healthcare quality and administrative practices. Dr Barot Served as the Chief Hospital Administrator at SAVA Multispeciality Hospital, Surendranagar (Gujarat), with a focus on accreditation, planning, and quality excellence. His leadership contributed to the hospital's recognition as a top healthcare facility in the region, with a 4.3-star rating based on patient reviews. Throughout his career, Collaborated with over 200 hospitals as a consultant for accreditation, new hospital planning and design, quality assurance, legal compliance, cashless empanelment, healthcare IT, and infrastructure consulting in accordance with relevant regulations.

DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP having Extensive track record of successfully completed over 150 hospitals NABH accreditation & Certification projects. Completed over 25 Hospitals planning and design projects. facilitating with more than 50 Hospitals TPA & Health Insurance Companies empanelment. Additionally, adept at securing government scheme tie-ups for 10 hospitals and achieving Silver certification for 5 others.
