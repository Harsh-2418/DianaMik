About DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP

DianaMik Hospital Management Consultancy LLP was founded on 16th August 2018 by Dr. Hardik V Barot, a qualified hospital management professional. After completing his studies in 2016, Dr. Barot embarked on a career dedicated to enhancing healthcare quality and administrative practices.

He served as the Chief Hospital Administrator at SAVA Multispeciality Hospital, Surendranagar (Gujarat), focusing on accreditation, planning, and quality excellence. His leadership helped the hospital earn a 4.3-star rating based on patient reviews.

Over his career, Dr. Barot has collaborated with 200+ hospitals as a consultant for accreditation, hospital planning & design, quality assurance, legal compliance, cashless empanelment, healthcare IT, and infrastructure consulting aligned with current healthcare regulations.
