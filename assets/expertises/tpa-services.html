About TPA & Health Insurance Empanelment

In today’s healthcare ecosystem, TPA (Third Party Administrator) and Health Insurance Empanelment play a vital role in ensuring cashless treatment, transparent billing, and timely reimbursements for patients.

Hospital Empanelment with leading insurance companies and TPAs enhances a hospital’s credibility, expands patient reach, and supports compliance with NABH, NABL & JCI standards.

What is TPA Empanelment?

A Third Party Administrator (TPA) is an authorized organization that manages health insurance claims and provides support for cashless hospitalization on behalf of insurance companies.

It allows hospitals to provide cashless treatment to insured patients while maintaining transparency, quality, and documentation standards required by Accreditation bodies.

Importance According to Accreditation Standards

Under NABH, NABL & JCI guidelines, organizations must ensure:

Transparency

Authenticity in billing and claim settlement.

Information

Availability of patient rights regarding insurance coverage.

Ethics

Ethical processes for admissions to discharges under insurance.

Grievance

Mechanisms for redressal related to insurance services.
