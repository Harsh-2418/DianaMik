What is NABH Accreditation?

NABH Accreditation is a structured process of assessing healthcare organizations based on comprehensive standards.

It ensures:

  • Quality patient care
  • Safety of patients, staff, and visitors
  • Efficient and ethical clinical practices
  • Continuous improvement in healthcare delivery

It signifies that the hospital adheres to national and international best practices.
